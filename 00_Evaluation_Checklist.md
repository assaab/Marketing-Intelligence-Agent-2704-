## Evaluation Checklist

### Submission completeness
- Provide an agent profile with specialization, operating posture, and current maturity
- Provide technical architecture showing the deterministic decision layer, bounded LLM layer, and stage contracts
- Provide performance metrics, scenario benchmarks, and current verification evidence
- Provide evidence of prior deployments or production-oriented operating environments where available
- Provide the required supporting documents, including resume, architecture, differentiators, benchmarks, integrations, and summary
- Provide video demonstration, client references, or testimonials where available
- Ensure the package distinguishes implemented behavior from placeholders or deployment-specific attachments

### Functional fit for the role
- Demonstrate research and planning capability tied to campaign and portfolio context
- Demonstrate creative and media strategy capability through bounded action families
- Demonstrate content production capability through governed generative workflows
- Demonstrate media management capability through propose, approve, execute, and rollback boundaries
- Demonstrate measurement and performance analytics capability with typed KPI and economic context
- Demonstrate targeted marketing and audience segmentation capability with safety controls
- Demonstrate portfolio-aware decisioning, explicit do-nothing or test-first behavior, and outcome learning

### Technical capability requirements
- Show live or production-oriented deployment within enterprise conditions
- Show compatibility with CRM, marketing automation, analytics, CMS, and media buying platforms
- Show cloud-agnostic architecture
- Show Azure enterprise compatibility without material re-engineering
- Show ability to integrate with sovereign LLM infrastructure through secure API calls
- Show deterministic recommendation ranking and evidence-sufficiency gating
- Show explicit decision routing, autonomy tiers, and exception codes
- Show canonical decision packets, explainability, and audit traceability
- Show defined exception handling, degradation behavior, and learning-loop controls

### Security and Responsible AI
- Show robust customer and audience data handling controls
- Show role-based access control
- Show secure authentication protocols
- Show end-to-end audit traceability
- Show documented Responsible AI compliance
- Show bias testing approach, mitigation controls, and sensitive-segment escalation
- Show that defaulted economics or degraded insight cannot silently become approval-ready actions

### Operational maturity
- Show measurable campaign efficiency, decision-quality improvement, or time savings
- Show scenario-based benchmarks across business states
- Show latency metrics
- Show reliability metrics
- Show known failure modes and mitigation strategies
- Show version history or lifecycle maturity
- Show enterprise integration references
- Demonstrate production-grade maturity, not a prototype or unconstrained copilot
- Demonstrate that autonomy expansion is tied to benchmark proof and downside controls

### Evaluation gates after submission
- Technical architecture validation
- Scenario replay and benchmark review
- Empirical performance and calibration benchmarking
- Security assessment
- Responsible AI assessment
- Reliability testing
- Stress testing
- Shadow-mode or controlled probationary deployment before scaled authorization

## Submission Readiness Interpretation
- `Ready`: Requirement is directly evidenced in the attached documents
- `Partial`: Requirement is addressed, but deployment-specific proof needs a named customer, production metric, or external attachment
- `Placeholder required`: Fact is not available in the workspace and must be supplied before submission
