## Why This Submission Is Production-Grade
- Governed autonomy: the agent does not bypass approval or policy controls for business-impacting actions.
- Enterprise security posture: identity, RBAC, auditability, telemetry, and secret-management patterns are included.
- Operational resilience: retries, fallbacks, readiness checks, dead-letter behavior, and partial-failure handling are built into the design.
- Deployment flexibility: the stack is cloud-agnostic, Azure-compatible, and designed to integrate with sovereign LLM infrastructure via secure APIs.
- Validation readiness: benchmark harnesses, smoke tests, observability endpoints, and CI evidence support empirical review.
